Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

01

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions — solely to provide the service to you.

02

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to operate and improve Spendly. We use your email to identify your account and send essential service communications. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the expense tracking service.

03

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We do not use third-party advertising networks. Your expense data remains private and is never disclosed to outside parties except where required by law.

04

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorised systems only. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

05

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may delete your account and all associated data by contacting us. We will action your request promptly.

06

Cookies

Spendly uses session cookies strictly to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party cookies.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

08

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on our website.
